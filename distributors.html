Our Distributors
In order to become a Rangoon Chemical Works distributor, it just takes a little ambition, drive and determination. RCW distributors are usually pharmacy wholesale distribution companies who have the ability to supply pharmacies across Maharashtra, Gujarat and other states. The other requirement is that our distributors are able to deliver outstanding performances when it comes to service and customer-care.

We invite distributors to supply our products, the country-over. In order to become a distributor, one needs to fill out a form and enter his details, as provided on this website. Our Sales Team looks into these details and then scrutinizes them. Upon passing the scrutiny, the interested distributor is contacted. Distributors are selected on first call, first send basis.
